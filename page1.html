Syllabus

  • Quantitative Ability
    Fundamentals, Number Theory, Percentages, Profit and Loss, Averages, Simple and Compound Interest, Ratio and Proportion, Partnerships, Mixtures & Allegations, Time speed Distance, Geometry, Co-ordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, Mensuration, Venn Diagrams, Probability, Permutation and Combination, Races and Tracks, Time and Work, Pipes and Cisterns, Logarithms and Exponents, Clocks and Calendar, Inequalities, Inequalities and polynomials, Functions, Set Theory, Linear Quadratic Equation, Progression.
  • Data Interpretation
    DI basic terms and Graphs , PIE Charts, Tables, Grids and Tables, Word Problems, Tournaments and Visualization, Puzzles.
  • Current Affairs with General Knowledge
    Important Days, Indian History, Books and Authors, Indian National Movement, Awards and Honors, Sports, General Polity, Countries & Capitals, Science & Technology, Science – Inventions & Discoveries etc, National and International Current Affairs.
  • Logical Reasoning
    Blood Relation, Distance and Direction, Coding and Decoding, Binary Logic, Number series, Seating Arrangements, Cubes and Dices, Syllogisms, Non Verbal Reasoning, Input and Output, Statements and Conclusions, Statements and Assumption, Problems on Ages, Mirror and Water Images, Ordering and Ranking, Data Sufficiency, Analogies. 
  • Verbal Ability
    Noun, Pronoun, Preposition, Verb, Adverb and Conjunction, Direct and Indirect speech, Figures of speech, Phrasal Verbs, Tenses, Articles, Etymology & Root, Active/passive voice, Subject verb Agreement, Synonyms/ Antonyms, Sentence completion, Sentence Improvement, Fill in the blanks, Odd one out, One word substitutions, Idioms and Phrases, Spelling errors and Error Spotting, Para Jumbles, Analogies, Deductive Reasoning.
